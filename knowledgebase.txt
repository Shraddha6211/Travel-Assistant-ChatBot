Welcome to Wanderlust Travel Assistant Knowledge Base.

Destinations:
- Paris: Famous for Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Seine River cruises.
- Tokyo: Known for cherry blossoms, Shibuya Crossing, and traditional temples.
- New York: Highlights include Statue of Liberty, Central Park, and Broadway shows.
- Bali: Tropical beaches, rice terraces, and surfing spots.

Travel Tips:
- Always carry a copy of your passport and travel documents.
- Check visa requirements for each destination.
- Travel insurance is recommended for international trips.
- Local customs vary: research cultural norms before visiting.

Flights:
- Major airlines: Emirates, Qatar Airways, Singapore Airlines, Delta.
- Booking in advance can save up to 30% on fares.
- Flexible tickets allow date changes with minimal fees.

Accommodation:
- Hotels: 3-star, 4-star, 5-star available.
- Hostels and Airbnb are budget-friendly options.
- Check reviews before booking for quality assurance.

Local Transportation:
- Paris: Metro, buses, taxis, bike rentals.
- Tokyo: Subway, buses, taxis, trains.
- New York: Subway, taxis, buses, Uber/Lyft.
- Bali: Scooters, taxis, car rentals.

Currency:
- Paris: Euro (EUR)
- Tokyo: Japanese Yen (JPY)
- New York: US Dollar (USD)
- Bali: Indonesian Rupiah (IDR)

Emergency Contacts:
- Local police: 112 (EU), 110 (Japan), 911 (US), 112 (Bali)
- Embassy contacts recommended for international travelers.

Fun Facts:
- Paris is called "The City of Light."
- Tokyo is the largest metropolitan area in the world.
- New York City was the first capital of the United States.
- Bali is known as the "Island of the Gods."
